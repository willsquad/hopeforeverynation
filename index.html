Bringing Hope through Christ

Brett and Cathy Hoelzer are living in Doro, Republic of South Sudan, which is in Mabaan County, in the Upper Nile State. They have been serving together since 2006 in South Sudan. Their skills afford them the opportunity to meet both the physical and spiritual needs of the people in this region. Brett is busy managing multiple construction projects. He is also very busy discipling church leaders and new believers. As a physician assistant Cathy is directing the myriad of health programs associated with the Grieve Memorial Clinic and has many opportunities to share Christ with her patients.

With the ability to speak Arabic, both Brett and Cathy love reaching out to the thousands of Arabic speaking refugees that surround them in the refugee camps.
